HSL Value

hsl(hue, saturation, lightness)


Hue(명도) is a degree on the color wheel from 0 to 360.
0 is red 240 is blue

Saturation(채도) is a percentage value, 0% means shade of gray, 100% is the full colour

Lightness(밝기) - percentage, 0% is black 100% is white.
